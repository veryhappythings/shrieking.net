Woop, I'm in Boston.  It's a really cool city, much nicer than NYC.  I can't really afford to do anything after the extravagance of the last few weeks, but I've been very happy just wandering around the city or sitting in the park and reading.  Boston has a massive Irish population, which is weird - last night I went out with some Dutch guys to check out the Irish bars.  I miss the pubs back home.

It's been 2 weeks.  It feels like months to me, I can't quite believe it's only been that long. I wonder what the longest I've ever been away from Britain is: it can't be more than 2 and a half weeks.  I don't think the vastness of the time that I'm out here for has sunk in yet, I'm sure I'll get terribly homesick when it does!  For now, though, I'm doing alright.

I must dash, I'm late for my appointment with a book and a park.
